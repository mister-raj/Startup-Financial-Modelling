1. Assumptions Drive Everything

3. Working Capital = The Silent Driver
The model captures:

Days before inventory converts to sale

Days before receivables convert to cash

Days before payables must be paid

This timing mismatch determines how much cash the business eats before it generates cash.

4. Two Valuation Lenses

DCF: "What is this business worth based on its ability to generate future cash?"

Market Multiples: "What are investors paying for similar companies today?"
